<strong><strong>By Type of Care</strong></h4>
The Type of Care segment is divided into Preventive Care, Curative Care, Palliative Care. Preventive Care includes services and products that help individuals maintain their health and prevent the onset of illness or injury. Curative Care includes services and products that help individuals recover from illness or injury. Palliative Care includes services and products that help individuals manage symptoms and improve their quality of life during a serious illness or at the end of life. This Segments can help companies and healthcare providers better understand and target specific patient populations and tailor their products and services accordingly.
<strong><strong>By Devices</strong></h4>
The Devices segment is separated into Diagnostic &amp; Monitoring Devices, Therapeutic Devices, Home Mobility Assist Devices. In this Market Diagnostic and monitoring devices are increasingly being used in home healthcare settings as they enable patients to self-monitor their health conditions and avoid unnecessary hospitalization. These devices also help healthcare providers to remotely monitor patients and provide better care. Therapeutic devices are increasingly being used in home healthcare settings as they enable patients to receive treatments in the comfort of their own home, which can improve the patient's quality of life and reduce the burden on the healthcare system. Also home mobility assist devices are increasingly being used in home healthcare settings as they enable individuals with mobility issues to maintain their independence, safety and improve their quality of life. These devices also help healthcare providers to provide better care and support to patients with mobility impairments.
